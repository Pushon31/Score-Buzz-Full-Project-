SCORE BUZZ

SCORE BUZZ is one of the world's leading cricket analysis platforms and among the top ten single-sport platforms globally.

Cricket has always been a game of digits and SCORE BUZZ thrives on the ability to dig into those numbers — improving performance, studying the broader business of cricket, and contributing to the economics of the game through powerful analytics.

Founded in 2025, SCORE BUZZ delivers live ball-by-ball stats of all Test, ODI, T20I, and club matches. The app features extensive coverage, including live commentary, scorecards, fixtures, player rankings, team rankings, news, and much more. It also provides in-depth statistics on every match and every cricketer to have played the game.

With a long-term ambition to become the world’s most popular digital sports platform, SCORE BUZZ aims to drive competitive, entertaining, and constructive engagement with cricket for players, enthusiasts, and stakeholders alike.
